Dr. Nim – Game Rules

1.The game starts with a certain number of marbles (for example, 12).
2.On each turn, you or Dr. Nim can take 1, 2, or 3 marbles from the pile.
3.Players take turns removing marbles.
4.The player who takes the last marble wins the game.
5.The player who takes the last marble wins the game.

Tip:
Try to find a pattern in how Dr. Nim plays.
If you can predict his moves, you might discover the secret to always winning!
